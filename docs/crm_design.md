# CRM Design Overview

This CRM supports youth-serving organizations by providing:
- A single source of truth for youth profiles
- Full interaction history
- Clear tracking of needs and outcomes

## Table Roles
- youth_master: One record per youth
- conversation_log: One record per interaction
- needs_support: Tracks support and outcomes

## Design Principles
- Simple
- Searchable
- Scalable
- Tool-agnostic
